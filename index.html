  3. Fraud Transaction Detection

    May 2024

    This model is responsible for detecting fraudulent transactions. In this model, I have utilized a fraud transaction dataset which consists of 11 attributes and around 7 million rows. After performing EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis) including Statistical Analysis and data cleaning/wrangling on the dataset, I applied SMOTE technique to balance the dataset, since the target variable was highly imbalanced.
